## What's This About?

Hey! We're two high school students who love pets and coding. We noticed that pet owners don't really have their own dedicated space online to connect, share, and help each other out. So we built PetConnect - think of it as Instagram + TownSquare + ChatGPT but just for pet parents! ğŸ•ğŸˆ

## The Problem We're Solving

Pet owners are scattered across different apps and groups. You're searching Facebook for lost pet info, asking on Reddit about weird symptoms, scrolling Instagram for cute pics, and trying to find local meetups on random websites. We thought, "Why not put everything in ONE place?" So we did!
